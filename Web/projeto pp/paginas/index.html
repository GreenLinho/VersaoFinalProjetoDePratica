About Us

GreenBeam is a virtual market develloped by Matheus Seiji Luna Noda and Nícolas Maisonnette Duarte and idealized on September, 2019. As the main theme of this project, we decided to focus on something we both love, video games.
Basically, we wanted to make something that made the process of buying enjoyable with a nice and light design, fast search mechanics and shortcuts. That's what mainly moved our development, so we hope you love this experience as much as we loved to make this website for you!
